Student Life

Academic Life

Students at Kamelilo Day Secondary School experience a supportive academic environment with dedicated staff, structured lessons, and access to ICT and library facilities.

Clubs & Societies

Students participate in various clubs and societies, nurturing skills, creativity, and teamwork.

Sports & Physical Activities

From football and volleyball to athletics and indoor games, students actively participate in interschool competitions and physical wellness programs.

Student Leadership

We nurture leadership through prefects, class reps, club heads, and student council elections, empowering learners with voice and responsibility.

Health & Wellness

The school provides counseling, basic medical attention, clean water, and sanitation for student well-being and mental health support.

Spiritual Life

Regular spiritual services and moral instruction help instill discipline, respect, and values among students of all faiths.
