Admission Requirements

To enroll at Kamelilo Day Secondary School, prospective students must meet the following:

  • KCPE Certificate with recommended grades
  • Birth Certificate
  • Transfer Letter (if applicable)
  • Passport-sized photos (2)
  • Filled admission form

How to Apply

Admission is open throughout the year subject to space availability. Follow these steps:

  • Download or collect an admission form
  • Fill the form and attach required documents
  • Submit in person or via our school email
  • Await confirmation and joining instructions

Why Choose Kamelilo?

Kamelilo Day Secondary School is a nurturing environment for learners to grow intellectually and morally. We offer:

  • Excellent academic performance
  • Qualified and committed teaching staff
  • Modern learning resources
  • Discipline, mentorship, and leadership programs
  • Affordable fees and inclusive culture
